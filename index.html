Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do you accept pupils of all religions?

    Yes! We respect all doctrines and we also schedule some time on the timetable to enable pupils of different religions to worship their God.

  • Will your school develop my child's talents?

    Yes! Besides academics, we also participate in co-curricular activities which help pupils to discover and develop their talents.

  • Does your school give out bursaries?

    We give out bursaries to special categories of pupils like those who are talented in music and sports.

  • Does your school teach basing on the National Curriculum?
  • How is the general academic performance at you school?
